About

Welcome to Mulberry Glump, a Lakeside retreat along the South Coast of Lake Victoria. . The Retreat has conceived as a glamorous camping site where local Kenyans can enjoy five star glamping treatment at affordable costs. We've are true to the authenticity of our original concept, like making our own beds and offering a simple farm-to-table menu. Unless you request we have elctronics, no TVs, Phones or elctronics in your room/tent. We're unique among glumps in Nyanza for our commitment to sustainability and empowering the wonderful local people who who live side by side with our facility.
